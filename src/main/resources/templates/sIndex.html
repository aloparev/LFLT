Stock-Picking Experiment

Inspired by Daniel Kahneman's "Thinking, Fast and Slow".

The Illusion of Validity

In chapter 20 "The Illusion of Validity" Kahneman argues, that there is no such thing as stock-picking skill, it is just an illusion. And people who believe otherwise while trading on the stock market are playing the game of luck.
